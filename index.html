About Us

Nyange Clay

Nyange Clay is the ultimate supplies store offering you a one stop shop for all your construction needs and aggregates. We are en route to become one of the largest construction supplies distributors based in Rwanda and for now we retail certified brick ten and row lock bond bricks. In the next coming year we will add an extensive range of products to our catalog from tiles to black sheeting/polythene plastic.

From the comfort of your home or work place you get to place orders and buy high quality green bricks - charcoal powder injected bricks and in addition Nyange Clay saves you money and time by offering you the bricks at the lowest prices and we offer instant deliveries after confirmation of payments.

Who we are

DM Solutions is an investment group interested in participating in the development of our country through the implementation of green projects which uplift the rural residents. We are a team of experienced individuals supported by a well structured and skilled workforce and we believe in the potential of clay brick manufacturing. We are ready to invest and apply our time and knowledge to develop the plant and to participate in the growth of Ngororero District.













Project Impact

The first phase project after implementation will create employment opportunities for 53 persons and the long term plan is to employ 200 to 300 people as other investments are made and the plant is expanded.
