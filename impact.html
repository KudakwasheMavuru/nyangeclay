About Us

Being There

Place yourself in the little community of Nyange, Ngororero District in Rwanda. Create a picture in your mind as I describe your new circumstance. Feel it.

There are less than 5000 people in this community whose main source of income is agriculture. Almost 90% of the population falls under the categories D and E of the Ubudehe stratification programme. On a district level, the overall employment rate is 75.9% while the unemployment rate is 24.1%. Concerning industrialization, the district only counts 2 large main industries, one in the area of tea production and the other one in the mining sector in which you can be employed. The challenges of poverty, hunger and nutrition are endemic. A huge number of families, more than 65%, are living in extreme poverty.

Now the community is looking to take even more control of their lives and create economic activity to create jobs and build the infrastructure that they need the most such as schools , hospitals , mini-industrial parks, etc .The challenges of poverty, hunger and nutrition are endemic. Why Us

The advantages of VSBK Technology

Energy Efficiency

Very high-energy efficient method of firing bricks resulting in an energy saving of 30 % compared to BTK and more than 50% compared to clamps.

Environmental Impact

VSBK as a technology is much less emissive while considering not only GHG emissions but also in terms of SPM, SOx, NOx etc

Social Performance

Through incorporation of chimneys, mechanization of green brick lifting and brick loading process, it is possible to minimize occupational health risk and drudgery for workers.
